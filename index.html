About

Hi, Joseph Madril here! I design things for fun here on the web and dabble in some photography. During high school, I became interested in graphic design and basic programming. Although I planned on working in the tech field, I ultimately enrolled in college for a degree in Secondary Education teaching English. Only taking a semester of courses, I decided to take a few years away from school and re-evaluate my career path.

While my career today is away from programming and design, from time-to-time, I still create things for friends or just for fun as a hobby. Other hobbies include photography, music, reading and growing plants.
